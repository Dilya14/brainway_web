Solutions

Welcome! We are Brainway: a software house that specializes
in developing software solutions and providing technological
consulting to organizations.

Our development team is here from A to Z and is ready to empower your product
in every stage of production. We will work with you on product definition, an in-
depth analysis of your project’s needs to determine the technological means
and tools necessary to get the work done. Once we have the proper foundation
laid for your solution, we will then work through development and keep you
informed each step of the way. After completion, we strive to provide 24/7
support and maintenance services to ensure the product is fully operational
after deployment.

For Entrepreneurs & Start-Ups

We are here to accompany start-ups and entrepreneurs at every
stage from conception to solution production, PoC,
development, and initial product maintenance as it is exposed
to the market. We pride ourselves on this adaptive partnership
style because it gives you exactly what you need as a new
business to get your product created without the hassle of
working with multiple companies at each phase of creation.

Flexible Working Models

Having the flexibility to adjust the size of your team can be vital
when it comes time to divert your budget from R&D to
marketing or sales. With our adaptive approach, we will give you
the ability to adjust your development team and even add
part-time profiles like a CTO, Product Manager, or Chief
Architect, as necessary. We will adjust the team’s size to fit your
company’s current needs to offer you the expertise required to
keep your project running; this will help you avoid unnecessary
hiring and firing cycles and our experts will still remain available
to you whenever you need it.
